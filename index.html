Welcome to Traval Srilanaka

Sri Lanka offers captivating travel experiences, including water sports, diverse landscapes, cultural celebrations, and thrilling water rafting adventures. From scuba diving and kiteboarding to exploring ancient cities and sacred temples, the island caters to adventure seekers and culture enthusiasts alike. Its stunning scenery, ranging from tea plantations to mist-covered mountains, leaves visitors in awe. The white-water rafting experiences in Kitulgala provide an adrenaline rush amidst beautiful rainforests. With its rich history, vibrant traditions, and natural wonders, Sri Lanka invites travelers to discover the beauty and excitement of this tropical paradise.

Immerse yourself in lush greenery, encounter exotic wildlife, and witness breathtaking panoramic views. Celebrating Culture, History, and Devotion

The Sri Lanka Dalada Perahera is a magnificent cultural event that takes place annually in the city of Kandy. This grand procession showcases the sacred tooth relic of Lord Buddha, accompanied by traditional dancers, drummers, elephants adorned with vibrant attire, and beautifully decorated floats. It is a captivating spectacle that embodies the rich religious and historical heritage of Sri Lanka. Read More

Exploring Sri Lanka's Thrilling Water Rafting Adventures

Water rafting in Sri Lanka offers thrilling adventures amidst stunning natural beauty. From the Kelani River's rapids near Kitulgala to the untamed waters of the Mahaweli River in Kandy, you'll paddle through lush forests, cascading waterfalls, and vibrant wildlife.
